# Product Overview

MEX is an on-chain bank providing comprehensive cross-border payment services built on blockchain technology.

## About MEX

MEX is an on-chain bank designed to optimize cross-border payments and remittances for a global citizen user base. We are building banking without borders, specifically focused on serving emerging markets. As an on-chain bank, we combine the security and transparency of blockchain with the functionality of traditional banking.

### Our Mission

We are rethinking banking to optimize cross-border payments for global citizens. Founded by experts in blockchain, payments, and emerging markets, we are bringing on-chain efficiency to cross-border banking.

## Core Functionality

### Cross-Border Payments

Send money across borders using:
- **USDC (USD Coin)**: Stablecoin pegged to USD
- **USDT (Tether)**: Widely-used stablecoin
- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Traditional payment methods

### Key Features

- **Instant Settlement**: Transactions complete in minutes
- **Low Fees**: Under 1% transaction costs
- **Global Reach**: Send to recipients worldwide
- **24/7 Availability**: Access anytime, anywhere
- **Multiple Currencies**: Support for various fiat and digital currencies

## Product Details

### Technology Stack

MEX is built using modern technologies:

- **Frontend**: React.js with Carbon Design System
- **Blockchain**: Ethereum and Polygon networks
- **Smart Contracts**: Solidity-based payment processing
- **Stablecoins**: USDC and USDT integration

### Architecture

- **Decentralized**: Leveraging blockchain technology
- **Scalable**: Built to handle high transaction volumes
- **Secure**: Multi-layer security implementation
- **Transparent**: All transactions on public ledger

### Development

## Target Users

### Primary Users

- **Migrant Workers**: Sending remittances to families back home
- **Global Citizens**: People working and living across borders
- **Emerging Markets**: Users in regions with limited banking access
- **Businesses**: Companies needing efficient cross-border payments

## Use Cases

1. **Remittances**: Send money to family members in other countries
2. **Business Payments**: Pay international suppliers and contractors
3. **Personal Transfers**: Send money to friends and family abroad
4. **Emergency Funds**: Quick access to funds in urgent situations

## Competitive Advantages

- **Blockchain-Based**: Leveraging distributed ledger technology
- **Lower Costs**: Significantly cheaper than traditional banking
- **Faster Processing**: Minutes instead of days
- **Greater Accessibility**: No bank account required
- **Transparency**: All transactions recorded on blockchain
