# Use Cases

Real-world scenarios where MEX provides value.

## Remittances

### Scenario: Sending Money Home

**User**: Maria, a nurse working in the United States  
**Recipient**: Her family in the Philippines  
**Amount**: $500 monthly  
**Traditional Method**: 5-7 days, $40 fee (8%)  
**MEX**: Minutes, $5 fee (1%)

**Benefits**:
- Faster delivery of funds
- More money reaches recipients
- Lower overall costs
- 24/7 availability

## Key Advantages Across Use Cases

### Speed

#### Traditional Banking
3-7 days for cross-border transfers

#### MEX Platform
Minutes for transaction completion

### Cost

#### Traditional Banking
5-10% fees on transaction amounts

#### MEX Platform
Under 1% transaction fees

### Accessibility

#### Traditional Banking
Bank account required, operates during business hours

#### MEX Platform
Internet only, 24/7 availability

### Transparency

#### Traditional Banking
Opaque fee structures, limited transaction visibility

#### MEX Platform
Clear, blockchain-recorded transactions with full transparency
