# Investor Overview

MEX is building the future of on-chain banking. We're seeking strategic investors who share our vision of revolutionizing cross-border payments through blockchain technology.

## About MEX

MEX is an **on-chain bank** designed to optimize cross-border payments and remittances for a global citizen user base. We leverage blockchain technology to provide banking services without borders, specifically focused on serving emerging markets.

### Our Vision

To become the leading on-chain bank for cross-border payments, making financial services accessible, affordable, and efficient for millions of global citizens.
