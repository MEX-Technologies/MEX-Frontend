# Investor Contact

Get in touch with MEX's investor relations team.

## Contact Information

### Investor Relations
**Email**: investors@mex.money

### General Inquiries
**Email**: hello@mex.money

## Investment Inquiry

When reaching out, please include:

### Investor Information
- Firm name or individual name
- Investment focus and stage
- Portfolio companies (if applicable)
- Investment size range

### Interest Details
- Type of investment interest
- Strategic value you bring
- Timeline for investment decision
- Any specific questions or areas of interest

### Background
- Relevant experience
- Market expertise
- Previous investments in fintech/blockchain
- Strategic partnership opportunities

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all investor inquiries within 2-3 business days.

## What to Expect

### Initial Response
- Acknowledgment of your inquiry
- Initial information about MEX
- Next steps in the process

### Information Sharing
- Company overview and deck
- Financial information (under NDA)
- Technical documentation
- Market analysis

### Due Diligence
- Detailed financial information
- Technical deep-dive
- Market validation data
- Team introductions

## Confidentiality

All investment discussions are treated with strict confidentiality. We require NDAs for detailed financial and strategic information.

## Investment Process

### Step 1: Initial Contact
Reach out via email with your investment interest

### Step 2: Information Sharing
We share company information and answer initial questions

### Step 3: Due Diligence
Comprehensive due diligence process

### Step 4: Term Sheet
Investment terms and structure discussion

### Step 5: Closing
Legal documentation and fund transfer
