# Quick Start

Get up and running with MEX in minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Internet connection
- Valid email address
- Supported web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Join the Waitlist

Visit our homepage and join the waitlist to get early access to MEX.

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

Check your email for a verification link and confirm your account.

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

Set up your profile with basic information required for transactions.

### Step 4: Add Payment Method

Connect your preferred payment method:
- USDC (USD Coin)
- USDT (Tether)
- Credit/Debit Cards

### Step 5: Start Sending

You're ready to send cross-border payments!

## First Transaction

1. Navigate to the "Send Money" section
2. Enter recipient details
3. Select amount and currency
4. Choose payment method
5. Confirm and send

Your transaction will be processed on the blockchain and completed in minutes.
