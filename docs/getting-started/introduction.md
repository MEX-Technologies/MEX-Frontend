# Introduction

MEX is an on-chain bank that leverages blockchain technology to revolutionize how global citizens send and receive money across borders.

## What is MEX?

MEX is an on-chain bank designed to optimize cross-border payments and remittances for a global citizen user base. We are building banking without borders, specifically focused on serving emerging markets. As an on-chain bank, we provide traditional banking services powered by blockchain technology.

## Our Mission

We are rethinking banking to optimize cross-border payments for global citizens. Founded by experts in blockchain, payments, and emerging markets, we are bringing on-chain efficiency to cross-border banking.

## Key Principles

MEX is built on core principles that guide our development:

#### Accessibility

Banking services available to anyone with internet access

#### Efficiency

Near-instant settlement times compared to traditional banking

#### Affordability

Significantly lower fees than traditional cross-border transfers

#### Transparency

Blockchain-based transactions provide full transparency

#### Global Reach

Serve users in emerging markets and beyond

## Why MEX?

### Traditional Banking Challenges

Traditional cross-border payment systems are plagued by:

- **Slow Processing**: 3-5 business days for international transfers
- **High Costs**: 5-10% fees on transaction amounts
- **Limited Access**: Requires bank accounts and operates during business hours
- **Complex Infrastructure**: Multiple intermediaries adding delays and costs

### MEX Solutions

MEX addresses these challenges by leveraging blockchain technology to provide:

- **Instant Settlement**: Transactions complete in minutes, not days
- **Low Fees**: Under 1% transaction costs
- **24/7 Availability**: Accessible anytime, anywhere
- **Streamlined Process**: Direct peer-to-peer transactions
