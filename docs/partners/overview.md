# Partnership Overview

MEX is building the future of on-chain banking. We welcome strategic partnerships that align with our mission to revolutionize cross-border payments.

## About MEX

MEX is an **on-chain bank** designed to optimize cross-border payments and remittances for a global citizen user base. We leverage blockchain technology to provide banking services without borders, specifically focused on serving emerging markets.

### Our Mission

We are rethinking banking to optimize cross-border payments for global citizens. Founded by experts in blockchain, payments, and emerging markets, we are bringing on-chain efficiency to cross-border banking.

## Why Partner with MEX?

### Market Opportunity

- **Growing Market**: Global remittance market valued at over $800 billion annually
- **Emerging Markets**: High demand in regions with limited banking access
- **Blockchain Adoption**: Increasing acceptance of blockchain-based financial services
- **Digital Transformation**: Shift towards digital-first banking solutions

### Our Advantages

#### Technology Leadership

- **On-Chain Infrastructure**: Built on proven blockchain networks (Ethereum, Polygon)
- **Scalable Platform**: Designed to handle high transaction volumes
- **Security First**: Multi-layer security and compliance framework
- **Open Source**: Transparent, community-driven development

#### Market Position

- **First-Mover Advantage**: Early in the on-chain banking space
- **Emerging Markets Focus**: Specialized knowledge of target markets
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Operating with proper licenses and compliance
- **Strong Team**: Experienced in blockchain, payments, and emerging markets
