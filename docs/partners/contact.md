# Partner Contact

Get in touch with MEX's partnerships team.

## Contact Information

### Partnerships Email
**Email**: partners@mex.money

### General Inquiries
**Email**: hello@mex.money

## Partnership Inquiry

When reaching out, please include:

### Company Information
- Company name and website
- Industry and market focus
- Company size and location

### Partnership Interest
- Type of partnership you're interested in
- Specific use case or integration
- Target markets or regions

### Technical Details
- Current technology stack
- Integration requirements
- Timeline expectations

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all partnership inquiries within 2-3 business days.

## What to Expect

### Initial Response
- Acknowledgment of your inquiry
- Initial questions or information request

### Follow-Up
- Detailed discussion of partnership opportunity
- Technical requirements review
- Partnership proposal if aligned

## Partnership Criteria

We evaluate partnerships based on:

### Strategic Alignment
- Alignment with MEX's mission and values
- Market opportunity and potential
- Long-term strategic value

### Technical Feasibility
- Integration complexity
- Technical requirements
- Resource availability

### Regulatory Considerations
- Compliance requirements
- Regulatory approvals needed
- Risk assessment

## Confidentiality

All partnership discussions are treated with strict confidentiality.
